IIIT Vadodara Campus Master Plan

A Vision of Excellence and Sustainability

About the Master Plan

The Master Plan for IIIT Vadodara reflects a meticulous design crafted to inspire innovation and foster a dynamic academic environment. Spread across 62.5 acres, the campus is located alongside the scenic Vishwamitri River, integrating modern infrastructure with sustainable practices.

The plan includes key facilities such as Academic Blocks (G+5), Combined Hostel Blocks (G+9), and faculty and staff residences, ensuring a balanced and convenient lifestyle for students, faculty, and staff. A state-of-the-art health and fitness block, along with an open-air theatre and recreational zones, will nurture both mind and body.

The campus is divided into phases, each focusing on delivering world-class facilities. Phase-1 lays the foundation with core academic and residential buildings, while future phases envision holistic growth, incorporating advanced amenities for research, innovation, and extracurricular activities.

Designed with a focus on sustainability and accessibility, the campus will become a hub of technological advancement and a model of academic excellence for generations to come.
